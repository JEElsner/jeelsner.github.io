Jonathan Elsner

I'm at the University of Washington for my Master's Degree in Applied and Computational Mathematics. I'm passionate about technical ideas and telling other people about them. My interests include optimization, numerical analysis, and machine learning, and dynamical systems. Most recently, I was employed at the Allen Institute as a Workshop Specialist, in which I developed Jupyter Notebooks for a summer workshop on computational neuroscience using large datasets. When not studying, I can be found playing the Trombone, cooking, or taking photos with my Dad's Canon AE-1.
